    Actually, you get the data of the most probable situation of conflict resolution, including:

  • Q: How does this work?

    A: The conflict simulation is based on compatible strategies (see table). The assumptions behind the algorithm are the following:

    • All conflicts are resolved.
    • Conflicts are non-zero-sum games.
    • Players are motivated to solve the conflict.
    • Players change their strategy if needed, strategy choices are made by individual preferences.
    • A conflict is resolved if all player strategies are compatible with each other.
